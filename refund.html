Refund Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

BQ Tools is a digital service that delivers AI-generated content instantly upon use. Please review our refund policy below before making a purchase.

Used Credits Non-Refundable

Credits are consumed immediately when an AI action is processed. Once a credit has been used to generate a report, estimate, analysis, or chat response, it cannot be refunded.

Unused Credit Packs Non-Refundable

Credit packs are digital goods delivered immediately upon purchase. As per California Civil Code and standard digital goods practices, we do not offer refunds on purchased credit packs, even if credits remain unused.

Pro Subscription Cancel Anytime

You may cancel your Pro subscription at any time. Your Pro benefits and monthly credits continue until the end of the current billing period. No refund is issued for the remaining days of the current period. No future charges will occur after cancellation.

Technical Issues Case-by-Case

If a technical issue on our side prevents you from receiving the AI output you paid for (e.g., server error during processing, credits deducted but no result delivered), please contact us. We will either:

  • Restore the credits that were lost due to the technical issue, or
  • Re-process your request at no additional cost

Cryptocurrency Payments Non-Refundable

Due to the nature of blockchain transactions, cryptocurrency payments cannot be reversed or refunded. Please ensure you are sending the correct amount to the correct wallet address before confirming your transaction.

How to Request Support

If you believe you are entitled to a credit restoration due to a technical issue, contact us at:

Email: fanzai.mgmt@gmail.com

Include your account email, the date and time of the issue, and a description of what happened. We aim to respond within 48 hours.
